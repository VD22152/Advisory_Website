What's Plastic Pollution?

Plastic pollution is a problem that involves plastic being left and wasted into our environment causing damage and mishaps to animals and the lands. Having these plastics lying around our environment is a threat to our wildlife and ecosystem which is a major problem in our lands.

Why Does This Happen?

Plastic pollution is a big problem in our environment as it’s a problem that is difficult to find an exact solution for. Plastic is something that doesn’t degrade and decompose due to its complicated chemical formula and properties, this makes plastic highly difficult to get rid and expose of. This is plastic is recycled rather than destroyed.
