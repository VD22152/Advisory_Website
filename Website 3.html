Problems

Some problems that plastic pollution cause is harm to our marine life, damage to our soil and ground water, harm to humans, reduced ability to adapt to climate change, and impact on our livelihoods. All these problems all have to do with plastic pollution, and this is why our lands must be protected and kept clean from plastic.
